Global Village Coffeehouse Map Style

The Global Village Coffeehouse aesthetic was prevalent during the late 1980s through the early 2000s. Being a nostalgic person, I felt this was the perfect aesthetic to create a basemap for. I have always been a fan of its eccentric yet cozy feel. I explain my design choices in the following scroll map, discussing map elements such as color, texture, form, and typography.
